# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

As participants, we pledge to foster a respectful environment. We aim for a community that is open to everyone regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, experience level, religion, sexual orientation, or similar.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contribute to a positive environment:

- Using respectful language
- Welcoming different viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive feedback
- Taking responsibility for the community
- Showing empathy toward other participants

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Violent or threatening language or behavior
- Discriminatory, harassing, or insulting comments
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without consent
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional context

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in project spaces (issues, PRs, Discussions, email, etc.) and at public events related to the project.

## Enforcement

If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please contact the project maintainers. All reports will be reviewed and may result in appropriate action.
